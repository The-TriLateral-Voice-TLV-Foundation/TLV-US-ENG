## ◉ What is TLV?

**TriLateral Voice (TLV)** is a comprehensive communication framework that documents and honors the full dimensionality of human experience. Unlike traditional communication approaches that focus solely on words, TLV systematically attends to three integrated dimensions:

| Voice | Focus | What It Captures |
|-------|-------|-----------------|
| **◉ Speaker's Voice** | *What you say* | Technical content, verbatim words, factual information, narrative structure, expressed intentions |
| **◎ Emotional-Somatic Voice** | *How it feels* | Primary emotions, physical sensations, intensity levels, authenticity indicators |
| **◈ Observer's Voice** | *Why it matters* | Cultural/historical context, pattern recognition, temporal factors, meta-awareness |

TLV provides structured protocols for transcription, translation, interpretation, conflict mediation, discourse mediation, biography, and authentic communication — ensuring that the complexity of human expression is preserved with dignity, clarity, and respect.

> **Why Three?** Because without the witness, communication remains incomplete. We are not only speakers and feelers — we are also witnesses to our own process and, through that, the process of others.

## ◎ Mission

> *To document and honor the full dimensionality of human communication, recognizing how environment, experience, and the need for authentic self-expression shape what we say, how we feel, and who we are.*

## ◈ What's New in RC0001.1

RC0001.1 represents the evolution of TLV from **protocol specification** (RC0001) to **operational system** (RC0001.1), adding critical infrastructure for interpretation methodology, interrogation methods, implementation guidance, foundational philosophy, and organizational clarity.

- **Genesis Protocol** — Philosophical foundation explaining why TLV exists and the problem it solves
- **Builders Manual** — Complete implementation guide for system builders
- **Interrogation Methods** — 4 SOPs for systematically surfacing deeper meaning (general, collaborative, self-interrogation, and interrogation of thought)
- **Interpretation Protocol** — Retroactive TLV analysis of existing speech, text, and historical documents
- **Biography Protocol** — Methodology for creating TLV biographies, proven by 3 delivered biographies
- **Discourse Mediation & Support Protocol** — Extended framework for complex multi-party discourse
- **TLV Introduction** — Unified three-voiced introduction replacing separate White Paper, Blue Paper, and Common Speech documents
- **4-Language Pilot Translations** — Vocabulary translations in French, Spanish, German, and Standard Mandarin Chinese
- **Schema Priming SOP** — Phase-zero schema construction before research or deconstruction
- **Methods for Effective Research SOP** — Schema-integrated, evidence-based research workflow
- **Methods for Narrative Deconstruction SOP** — Eleven-phase method for deconstructing narratives and media
- **Organizational Restructuring** — New directories (Deliverables, Dictionaries, Manuals, Records, Samples, SOPs, Templates) for clearer separation of concerns

## ◎ Applications

TLV can be applied across diverse contexts:

- **Personal Life** — Self-reflection, journaling, understanding communication patterns
- **Relationships & Family** — Improving clarity, processing conflict, deepening connection
- **Healthcare & Therapy** — Person-centered care, documenting patient experiences (as complement, not replacement)
- **Education** — Teaching communication complexity, developing emotional intelligence
- **Workplace** — Clear organizational communication, conflict understanding, leadership development
- **Research** — Qualitative methodology, phenomenological studies, preserving authentic voices
- **History & Legacy** — Oral history projects, generational storytelling, cultural documentation, biographical analysis
- **Legal** — Courtroom testimony transcription with emotional context preserved

## ◈ Implementation Paths

RC0001.1 formalizes three implementation paths:

| Path | Best For | Complexity | Timeline |
|------|----------|------------|----------|
| **A: Software-Integrated** | Video platforms, court systems, healthcare EMR | High | 6–12 months |
| **B: Workflow-Layer** | Organizations with existing transcription workflows | Medium | 2–4 months |
| **C: Manual / Hybrid** | High-stakes contexts (legal, diplomatic, archival) | Low technical, High training | 1–2 months |

> See the [Builders Manual](Manuals/) for detailed guidance on each path.

## ◎ Foundational Principles

TLV is grounded in three key axioms:

> **Axiom 1** — *A past we have survived is not a defect; it is a victory we need to claim.*

> **Axiom 2** — *Life is the reward for surviving our past.*

> **Axiom 3** — *No one can make you mad — it is an internal response to external input.*

These principles emphasize agency, resilience, and the recognition that emotional responses are internal processes influenced by — but not determined by — external events.

---

## ◈ Important Scope Clarification

**TLV is NOT therapy or clinical treatment.** TLV documents experience — it does not diagnose, treat, or provide mental health care. While TLV methods can inform care and support authentic understanding, they do not replace professional mental health services, medical treatment, or clinical therapy.

If you're experiencing persistent emotional distress, thoughts of self-harm, trauma responses, or mental health crisis, please reach out to appropriate resources: mental health professionals, crisis hotlines, trusted support networks, or medical providers.
