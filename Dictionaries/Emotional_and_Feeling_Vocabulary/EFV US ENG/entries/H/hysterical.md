- **Original Meaning**: Related to womb; historically attributed to uterine disturbance and extreme emotional states
- **Semantic Evolution**: Uterine attribution → extreme emotion → contemporary uncontrolled emotional state and overwhelming reactivity
- **Cultural Shifts**: Uterine attribution → extreme emotion → uncontrolled state → balance understanding
