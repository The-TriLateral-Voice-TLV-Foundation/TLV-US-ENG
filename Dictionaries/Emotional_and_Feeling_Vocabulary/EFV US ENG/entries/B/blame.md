#### 1. Etymology & Historical Development
- **Root Language**: Old French "blasmer" from Latin "blasphemare" (to blaspheme, speak evil of)
- **Original Meaning**: To speak evil of, to curse or condemn; to hold responsible
- **Semantic Evolution**: Religious blasphemy → moral condemnation → responsibility assignment → contemporary fault-finding
- **Cultural Shifts**: Religious offense → moral judgment → legal responsibility → psychological defense mechanism
- **Somatic Etymology**:
  - Original Physical Meaning: Verbal attack and condemnation with pointing and accusatory gestures
  - Embodiment Evolution: Religious condemnation → moral judgment → defensive accusation
  - Cultural Somatic Shifts: Sacred condemnation → moral judgment → psychological projection
