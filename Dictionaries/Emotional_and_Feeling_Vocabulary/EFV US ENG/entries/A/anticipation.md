- **Root Language**: Latin "anticipatio" from "anticipare" (to take before) from "ante" (before) + "capere" (to take)
- **Original Meaning**: Taking or acting beforehand; expecting or looking forward
- **Semantic Evolution**: Acting beforehand → expecting → emotional looking forward → contemporary excited expectation
- **Cultural Shifts**: Military/strategic preparation → general expectation → emotional excitement
- **Somatic Etymology**:
