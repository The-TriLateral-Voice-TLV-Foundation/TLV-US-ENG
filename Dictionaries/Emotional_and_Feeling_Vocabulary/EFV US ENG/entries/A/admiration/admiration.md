#### 1. Etymology & Historical Development
- **Root Language**: Latin "admīrātio" from "admīrārī" (to wonder at) from "ad-" (at) + "mīrārī" (to wonder)
- **Original Meaning**: Wonder or amazement at something remarkable
- **Semantic Evolution**: Wonder/amazement → respect for excellence → positive regard with aspiration
- **Cultural Shifts**: Religious awe → artistic/intellectual appreciation → contemporary role model respect
- **Somatic Etymology**:
  - Original Physical Meaning**: Physical response to wonder - eyes widening, breath catching
  - Embodiment Evolution: Awe response → aesthetic appreciation → inspirational uplift
  - Cultural Somatic Shifts: Religious reverence posture → intellectual appreciation → inspirational energy
