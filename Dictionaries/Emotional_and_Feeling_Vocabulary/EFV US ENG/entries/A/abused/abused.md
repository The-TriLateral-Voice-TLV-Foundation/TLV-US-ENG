#### 1. Etymology & Historical Development
- **Root Language**: Latin "abūsus" from "abūtī" (to use up, misuse) from "ab-" (away) + "ūtī" (to use)
- **Original Meaning**: Improper or excessive use leading to harm or depletion
- **Semantic Evolution**: Misuse of resources → mistreatment of persons → trauma/victimization
- **Cultural Shifts**: Property concept → human rights violation → trauma-informed understanding
- **Somatic Etymology**:
  - Original Physical Meaning: Physical wearing down or damage through misuse
  - Embodiment Evolution: Resource depletion → personal violation → embodied trauma
  - Cultural Somatic Shifts: Visible injury → psychological harm → neurobiological impact
