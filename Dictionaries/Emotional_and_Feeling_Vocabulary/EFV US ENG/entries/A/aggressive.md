- **Root Language**: Latin "aggressus" from "aggredi" (to approach, attack) from "ad-" (to) + "gradi" (to step)
- **Original Meaning**: To step toward, approach with intent to attack or confront
- **Semantic Evolution**: Military attack → assertive behavior → contemporary forceful approach
- **Cultural Shifts**: Combat behavior → social dominance → achievement strategy
- **Somatic Etymology**:
