#### 1. Etymology & Historical Development
- **Root Language**: Latin "apprehendere" (to seize, grasp, understand) from "ad-" (to) + "prehendere" (to grasp)
- **Original Meaning**: To grasp mentally, understand, or anticipate
- **Semantic Evolution**: Mental grasping → understanding → fearful anticipation → contemporary anxiety
- **Cultural Shifts**: Intellectual comprehension → emotional anticipation → anxious expectation
- **Somatic Etymology**:
  - Original Physical Meaning: Physical act of grasping or reaching toward understanding
  - Embodiment Evolution: Mental grasping → emotional reaching → anxious anticipation
  - Cultural Somatic Shifts: Physical comprehension → emotional anticipation → anxiety response
