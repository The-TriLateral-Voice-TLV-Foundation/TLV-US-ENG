#### 1. Etymology & Historical Development
- **Root Language**: Old French "anoiance" (trouble, vexation) from "anoier"
- **Original Meaning**: The act of troubling or the state of being troubled
- **Semantic Evolution**: Serious trouble → social irritation → everyday frustration source
- **Cultural Shifts**: Major disturbance → minor social problem → personal irritation
- **Somatic Etymology**:
  - Original Physical Meaning: Source of physical trouble and disturbance
  - Embodiment Evolution: Major disturbance → minor irritation → manageable frustration
  - Cultural Somatic Shifts: Serious problem → everyday irritation source
