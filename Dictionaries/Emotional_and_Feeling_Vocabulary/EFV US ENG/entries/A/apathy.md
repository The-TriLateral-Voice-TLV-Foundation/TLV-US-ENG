- **Root Language**: Greek "apatheia" (without feeling) from "a-" (without) + "pathos" (feeling)
- **Original Meaning**: Freedom from emotion or passion; philosophical ideal of detachment
- **Semantic Evolution**: Stoic virtue → emotional numbness → modern disconnection syndrome
- **Cultural Shifts**: Ancient philosophical ideal → pathological condition → contemporary epidemic
- **Somatic Etymology**:
