#### 1. Etymology & Historical Development
- **Root Language**: Latin "competens" from "competere" (to strive together, be suitable)
- **Original Meaning**: Suitable, fitting, adequate for the purpose
- **Semantic Evolution**: Legal suitability → general adequacy → contemporary skill and effectiveness
- **Cultural Shifts**: Legal qualification → professional competence → personal effectiveness → life skills
- **Somatic Etymology**:
  - Original Physical Meaning**: Physical adequacy and suitability for required tasks
  - Embodiment Evolution**: Task suitability → skill demonstration → effective capability
  - Cultural Somatic Shifts**: Legal adequacy → professional skill → personal effectiveness
