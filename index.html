The Architecture
What Makes TLV Different

Standard communication captures one dimension. TLV captures three — simultaneously, in every utterance, every document, every protocol.

🗣️
Speaker's Voice
What You Say
The semantic content — words, facts, narrative, expressed intention. What standard transcription already captures. The technical layer.
🫀
Emotional / Somatic Voice
How It Feels
The felt, embodied experience — emotions, physical sensations, intensity, authenticity markers, the body's response to what is being communicated. What standard transcription discards.
👁️
Observer's Voice
Why It Matters
The meta-perspective — context, patterns, cultural significance, temporal connections, self-reflection. The witness function that makes meaning visible. What standard transcription cannot even represent.
